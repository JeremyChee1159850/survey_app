About Green Or Mean

Help Protect Aotearoa New Zealand's Biodiversity

At ConserveConnect, we believe in the power of collective action to protect and preserve our natural world. Our platform empowers conservation groups, charities, and passionate individuals to create and participate in engaging competitions that raise awareness for vital environmental causes.

  • Compete: Join or host conservation competitions such as “Tree of the Year” or “Coastline of the Year,” and rally support for the natural causes you care about.
  • Connect: Build a community around your cause by engaging with other users, sharing ideas, and fostering collaboration for conservation efforts.
  • Donate: Support your favorite causes directly through donations, helping fund crucial conservation projects and initiatives.

Join us in conserving today for a better tomorrow!
