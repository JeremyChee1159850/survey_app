Welcome to Green or Mean

The Green or Mean project is a short interactive survey designed to understand how everyday people choose plants, especially whether they are drawn more by appearance or environmental impact. You'll be shown pairs of plants and asked which one you prefer, with a quick question at the end to understand your reasoning. Your choices will help researchers explore public preferences and raise awareness about the dangers of invasive species in Aotearoa New Zealand. It is quick, anonymous and insightful, and at the end you will see a breakdown of how often you chose invasive versus non-invasive plants, helping you reflect on your own preferences and their potential ecological impact.
